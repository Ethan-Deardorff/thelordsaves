Communities

Reddit

This is a great way to ask questions, seek answers, and get support from people all around the world. From my own experience with this community, people are very quick to answer and very helpful.

Facebook

Great community with many people who are willing to help new Christians. Also some good encouragement in general.

Shereadstruth

A more blog-like page that has some explanations and readings of the Bible. This may be good for you if you just want to read instead of interact. It also has some more features like an app, a podcast, and study plans.

You should always look at your options when searching for answers online. There are a lot of biased pages that may lead you away from faith. The fact is we can only take the Bible as the most credible source. So if you need definite answers, look at the Bible and determine context of the verse you are curious about.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, simply ask us through our email or instagram. We do not judge and will not refuse to answer anyone’s messages. We are here to help everyone see God’s glory and want a change in their lives.
